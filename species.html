Where to Find Them

📍 Spanish Harbor Bridge — shadow lines

Work outgoing tide at bridge shadow lines. Present bait just upcurrent and let it swing into the structure.

Slot limit: Must be 28–33" to keep in Gulf waters. Check FWC regs for current season rules before keeping.
